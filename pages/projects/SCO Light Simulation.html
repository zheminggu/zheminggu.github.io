Light group behavior

To simulating hundreds of lights' behavior, we need a behavior system to help control behaviors. So I designed a light system to control grouped lights.

For each light, the fundamental behavior is rotating. However, how much degrees it needs to twist is difficult to calculate. So, I researched the light behaviors according to the video in the actual environment. I found that the light's behavior pattern can be divided into look at and back at a target (target here is a transparent object) when it comes to the large groups.

Then the question here is to control each light to look at or back at which target. Hence, the light system is to control lights behavior in sequence. I then designed some sequence behaviors to handle lights in the group.

  • ForLoopWithDelay: Change lights in sequence with time delay (Ex. turn off a light, wait 1 second then turn off next light ).
  • ReverseForloopWithDelay:Reverse the action of ForLoopWithDelay.
  • LoopChangeLight: Change light color, opacity in the loop.
  • LoopChangrLightBody:Change light position, rotation in the loop

Finally, control the lights with events in Levelsequences to correspond the time of a real show
