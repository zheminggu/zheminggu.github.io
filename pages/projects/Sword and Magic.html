Sword and Magic

This game is created with Unity Engine. I'm the team leader and art director of this project. I also developed the mission system for the game. This game is designed to meet the demand of our class (the Fundamental of Game Engine).

Sword and Magic

Background

*Google Chrome is preferred to play the video*

This is a universe composed of numerous space islands, this is a universe with the power of magic. Magicians can jump between space islands with portals. With the development, the space island I bornt becomes a beautiful kingdom. However, the resource is depleting gradually, my country also suffering. Until someday, a magician appears. He brings the news about promise land, which is very rich. If the resources there could be exploited, the kingdom can return to prosperity. I'm one of the best soldiers in my country. So the king orders me to be the assistant of the magician to find the promise land. We traveled lots of space island with the clue of the promise land. I can feel we are getting closer.

Technique Details

Project management

I'm the team leader of this project. My team has four members which are relatively small for building a game. So I divided this game project into four parts: animation, scene building, battle system, and mission system. Each member shouldered one part, all work it has done should be placed under the folder of its name. So, in the Assets, there are Adam (this is mine), HawkQ, Owl, Sheep.

  • Adam achieved the dialogue system (which is the mission system) and Minimap.
  • HawkQ realized the Inventory system and the battle system.
  • Owl designed animation such as Timeline and Cinemachine.
  • Sheep built the scene for the game.

If different parts have the intersection, members need to work together and place the work under the folder NameOneandNameTwo (such as Adam and HawkQ). The final game would be placed into the Common folder (all together).

Dialogue System

I created a dialogue system for this game, to manage missions and dialogues. With the power of ScriptableObject, it's easy to generate NPC dialogues. By dragging dialogue prefabs into another dialogue prefab, change the sequence of text is simple.
